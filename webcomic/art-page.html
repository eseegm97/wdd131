Ethan's Art Page Site Plan

Ethan Seegmiller

WDD 131-

Overview

Purpose

As an aspiring artist, I want to create a space that can be a home for my artwork and comics. I want it to be a space that people who want to see my art can find and navigate easily!

Audience

My audience will primarily be other art appreciators, comic fans, and potential employers or freelance clients.

Dynamic elements

Already, I know of a few dynamic elements that I want to add to my site, specifically related to art viewing. First of all, I would like for the home page of my site to contain a small portfolio conveyor near the bottom of the page, which will rotate automatically and contain many different works of art to catch the viewer's eye. Additionally, I want for there to be a form which visitors to the site can use to comission art works. From this, I would like there to be options to select different types of art comissions, and upon completing the form, it should automatically send a message to me letting me know to get started! I will use Javascript for both of these.
