Topographical Map

This is a topographical map. "Topographical" means that it represents the changes in elevation across the geography of the map. This is indicated by the lines present all along the map, which seem similar to rings on a tree. The closer the lines are together, the steeper and more severe the elevation in the land; the farther apart the lines are from one another, the elevation change is very gradual and subtle.
