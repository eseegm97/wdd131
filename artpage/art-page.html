Ethan Seegmiller

WDD 131-A2

Overview

Purpose

As a fan of art, one of my favorite artists is the surrealist painter, Salvador Dali. I want to create a space that can be a home for Dali's artwork. It will be a place where others can come and learn more about Salvador Dali and his art!

Audience

My audience will primarily be other art appreciators, and perhaps those learning about Dali in an art class or college course who want to learn more.

Dynamic elements

Already, I know of a few dynamic elements that I want to add to my site, specifically related to art viewing. First of all, I would like for the home page of my site to contain a small portfolio conveyor near the bottom of the page, which will rotate automatically and contain many different works of Dali's art to catch the viewer's eye. Additionally, I want for there to be a form which visitors to the site can use to ask questions about Dali's art and life. Additionally, it should automatically send a message to me letting me know there is a new message that needs a response. I will use Javascript for both of these.
